Professional Profile

Dr. P. Surendranath Reddy is a highly skilled Orthopaedic Surgeon with over 14 years of clinical, surgical, and academic experience. He specializes in Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy, and Trauma Surgery with a strong evidence-based, patient-centric approach.

He completed his MBBS from S.V. Medical College, Tirupati (2006), MS Orthopaedics from Kurnool Medical College (2011), and Fellowship in Joint Replacement at Srikara Hospital (2015). Currently, he serves as Associate Professor of Orthopaedics at PMRIMS.
